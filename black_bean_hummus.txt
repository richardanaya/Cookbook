
400g black beans beans (about 1 can)
3 tbsp water
1/2 cup of olive oil
1 lemon or 1/4 cup lemon juice
1 garlic clove
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika powder
1 tsp salt

1. Blend ingredients together.
2. Add a bit of water if the consistency is too thick.
3. Sprinkle a bit of paprika powder and drizzle good olive oil on top.
4. Serve cold on flat bread or with vegetables.
