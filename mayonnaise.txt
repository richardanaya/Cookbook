300 ml oil (neutral oil like peanut or macadamia)
3 egg yolks
1 tablespoon of dijon mustard

1. Blend egg yolks, and dijon breifly
2. Start adding oil very slowly to blender while blending on low
3. You might be able to tell this is working by sound
4. After all oil is added, add flavorings:

1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tabelspoon white vinegar
salt and pepper to taste
