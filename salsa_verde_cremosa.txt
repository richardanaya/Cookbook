Salsa Verde Cremosa

Blend and enjoy.  You can vary the heat with the amount of aji paste. This salsa goes amazing on everything: 

2 whole jalapenos with tops cut off
2 tablespoon aji amarillo pepper paste ( mexican grocery )
10 sprigs cilantro
2 tablespoons of some very mild white cheese ( queso fresca, cotija, parmasean )
1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
1/2 teaspoon salt
Juice of 1 lime
1/2 cup mayonaise
